When would you want to use a remote repository rather than keeping all your work local?
Usually when you need to share it with someone or you want to work in a different computer, like your work computer and your personal computer.

Describe the differences between forks, clones, and branches. When would you use one instead of another?
Fork is when you want to copy a repository online (from GitHub to your GitHub). Clone is useful when you want to copy repositories to your computer, no matter if it is from GitHub or another computer.
